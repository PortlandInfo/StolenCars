My Car was Stolen in Portland! What do I do next?

1. Call the police to start a police report

2. Call your insurance carrier to start a claim

3. Join the Facebook groups that help find stolen cars:

When my car was stolen, someone from one of these groups found it and messaged me with the location. These groups are really great and are comprised of volunteers who check known stolen vehicle dumping areas and run the plates to re-unite with owners.

Portland Stolen Cars
PNW Stolen Cars
Lost & Stolen in Portland

4. Search the known stolen car dump locations

** Be very careful when searching for your vehicle ** Many of these locations can be dangerous and you should use extreme caution.
